About Me

My academic work focuses on understanding gravitational theories and their implications for spacetime, cosmology and compact objects.

I am currently pursuing a master's degree in physics at LMU Munich. I am particularly interested in the mathematical structure of gravitational theories, analytical methods in general relativity and computational approaches to physical systems.
